Title: How to Style Colorful Knits for Spring 2026, According to Street Style

The colorful knit is spring’s easiest mood lifter, and we saw plenty of styles on the spring 2026 runways, adding to the playful mood of the season. From Prada’s unexpected color pairings to Chanel’s swinging textures, there was a palpable sense of joy on the catwalk. Translating that mood into everyday dressing, however, can feel less straightforward. Fortunately, the street style scene outside the most recent fashion weeks offered an easy answer.

Among fashion month’s best-dressed attendees, the statement sweater emerged as a reliable way to capture the season’s spirited attitude. With endless styling potential, the colorful knit has become a staple in the wardrobes of showgoers, including Vogue’s own Chloe Malle. Whether draped casually over the shoulders, worn in a striking Klein blue hue that has quietly been gaining traction, or used to sharpen an unexpected color combination, a well-placed knit instantly energizes an outfit and draws the eye.

Even for the minimalist, the silhouette remains effortlessly appealing—cozy, classic, and endlessly wearable—while a bold wash of color offers a subtle entry point into the season’s maximalist mood. It’s the balance of restraint and play that makes the piece so compelling, earning its place in your closet rotation.

Below, shop Vogue’s edit of colorful knits and discover how to style them.

Chloe Malle masters modern prep in Paris with a crisp blue button-up, tailored brown trousers, and a bright green thrown into the mix. Tucked and tailored, the look strikes that elusive balance—a go-to uniform that feels refined and anything but predictable.

Lean into the season’s maximalist mood by pairing a playful striped knit with a patterned skirt, courtesy of Dries Van Noten. For an added twist, finish the look with an unexpected slim loafer —bringing just the right amount of polish to the mix.

Klein blue has quietly been gaining traction as of late, delivering high impact in even the smallest doses. Spotted on Kendall Jenner as a sweeping scarf and at Celine in the form of slim lace-up shoes, the saturated shade makes a compelling case for subtle statements. Take Auralee’s bright-hued cardigan, worn effortlessly with a striped shirt, brown corduroys, and a woven shoulder bag.

Look no further than Michael Rider’s Celine for the ultimate colored knit inspiration. In the Resort 2026 collection, the brand masters the art of styling—making wardrobe essentials feel fresh again. Through thoughtful layering and unexpected color pairings, the colorful knit works overtime against your modern prep staples, proving that even the classics can feel entirely new.

For the minimalist, a pop of red offers just the right disruption to a neutral wardrobe. Casually drape a crimson sweater over your shoulders to instantly energize your everyday essentials. The result? A go-to uniform, revived with new life.

When you’re constantly on the go, your wardrobe should be as functional as it is fashionable. A brightly hued polo knit peeks out from beneath an elegant blazer, adding just the right amount of interest. Ground the look with a pair of worn-in jeans for an ensemble that carries you from morning to night, no changes required.
