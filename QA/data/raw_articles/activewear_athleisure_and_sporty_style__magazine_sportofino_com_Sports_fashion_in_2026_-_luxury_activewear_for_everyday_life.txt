Title: Sports fashion in 2026 - luxury activewear for everyday life

Sports fashion has long since ceased to function solely in the context of training. Today, physical activity is not just an hour in the gym, but a conscious lifestyle in which looking after fitness, health and well-being becomes a daily priority. This change is naturally reflected in fashion. Sporty elements have become a permanent part of urban styling, redefining the concept of comfort and modern elegance. Activewear has taken on a new meaning - it has ceased to be a functional accessory and has become a tool for expressing values and approaches to life. In 2026, athleisure will no longer be a trend, but the foundation of the modern wardrobe. It's fashion that keeps up with the pace of the day, combines aesthetics with comfort and reflects the growing need for a balance between activity and everyday life. Find out how to cleverly incorporate sporty wardrobe elements into urban fashion.

Sports fashion in 2026 will become even closer to us,. We are increasingly consciously slowing down and seeking balance in our daily lives. Overstimulation, noise fatigue and the pressure of constant availability make us turn to nature, silence and activities that promote mental wellbeing. Comfort, functionality and freedom of movement cease to be mere extras and become the basis of everyday choices. Sporty aesthetics inspired by outdoor movement and health concerns are naturally seeping into fashion, reflecting the need for harmony between body and mind. We have put together a compilation of some sporty trends for you to use not only during your workout, but also on a daily basis.

Do you appreciate tennis chic not only on the court? If so, how about tenniscore? It's a trend inspired by the tennis aesthetic that combines classic on-court outfits such as pleated skirts, polo shirts and tennis dresses with everyday wardrobe to create sporty-elegant looks that work well both out on the town and in casual, active ensembles. It features clean lines, a preppy look and sporty functionality, making it one of the season's hottest trends.

In the S'portofino range, you'll find tennis-inspired offerings from global brands, including Lacoste with its iconic polo shirts and tennis court-inspired dresses, and pieces from Bogner and Goldbergh, which fit perfectly with the trend's sporty-elegant aesthetic for a luxuriously sporty everyday look. High-quality materials, precision cuts and iconic cuts will work perfectly not only on court. Tenniscore is also part of a wider movement in fashion, where sporty functionality and elegance are combined in everyday styling. Tenniscore is a tribute to classic looks from the courts, transferred to the urban wardrobe, which is sure to be seen in 2026.

Do you value freedom in your everyday life? At work, meeting friends or travelling to the furthest corners of the world? Athleisure is one of the most important fashion trends of recent years. It perfectly combines sporty comfort with urban casual chic. These are clothes that you can really wear for many occasions. The name comes from a combination of the words athletic and leisure, which perfectly encapsulates the idea of the style - comfort above all else, but with a modern aesthetic. The athleisure style responds to the need for flexibility and well-being in all aspects of life, and its popularity is growing with the emphasis on health, balance and wardrobe functionality.

This style is also gaining popularity with celebrities and influencers who wear athleisure pieces not only to the gym but also in their everyday ensembles, further cementing its position as the “unofficial everyday uniform” of contemporary fashion. Athleisure combines the comfort and functionality of sportswear with urban, everyday chic. Stock up on comfortable leggings, sweatshirts, sneakers and tops that can be worn in many situations without sacrificing style. Major brands offer a wide range of athleisure items, such as Adidas by Stella McCartney or Deha and Varley, which combine sporty functionality with modern design. In addition, sneakers and urban-style shoes from brands such as On and Salomon complement the sporty part of your wardrobe perfectly.

Fashion perfectly reflects social and climate change, and quiet outdoor is an example of this. In the face of increasingly unpredictable weather and a changing climate, the importance of outdoor clothing that provides protection, functionality and comfort in a variety of conditions is growing - not only on mountain expeditions, but also in everyday, urban life. Good technical performance, breathable materials and resistance to wind or rain are now becoming a natural part of the modern wardrobe.

Quiet outdoor takes full advantage of the quiet luxury and gorpcore style, combining technology with refined simplicity. It is a minimalist, more subtle version of outdoor fashion, where there is no room for randomness. With a muted colour palette, clean forms, refined details and the highest quality workmanship, the garments are perfectly in tune with the aesthetics of a modern, conscious lifestyle. Quiet outdoor is fashion that offers functionality and durability. Global brands such as Peak Performance, Mammut and Amundsen Sports are well aware of the changes taking place in society and offer high-quality, functional clothing that keeps up with trends.

Gorpcore is another major trend, but also a lifestyle that draws inspiration from outdoor clothing and mountain expeditions. Its name comes from Good Old Raisins and Peanuts - a popular snack among trekkers - which strongly emphasises its functional, outdoor roots. Gorpcore reflects the growing trend of wearing comfortable, technical clothing with urban styling, without sacrificing aesthetics and quality. Functional boots, technical jackets, waistcoats, cargo trousers or fleece sweatshirts are becoming must-haves not only for the trail, but also for everyday wear.

Today, gorpcore is gaining importance as lifestyles change - the need for comfort, mobility and preparedness for changing weather conditions, as well as a growing interest in outdoor activities and contact with nature. It is a response to the fatigue of fast fashion and the exaggerated aestheticisation of fashion. Gorpcore focuses on practicality, durability and authenticity. It is also increasingly combining with a minimalist approach to wardrobe, where quality, function and versatility are what counts, with clothes that work both in and out of town.

Sports fashion doesn't have to be boring at all. On the contrary - in 2026 it will prove every day that luxury activewear combines comfort functionality and refined aesthetics. Climate change, the need to slow down and the increasing desire to be closer to nature mean that fashion is naturally evolving towards comfort, quality and conscious choices. Athleisure, tenniscore or quiet outdoors show that sporty silhouettes, technical materials and minimalist design are no longer reserved exclusively for physical activity and are increasingly becoming an integral part of the everyday wardrobe. Sports fashion in 2026 is a response to the real needs of a contemporary lifestyle that is more conscious and focused on wellbeing.
